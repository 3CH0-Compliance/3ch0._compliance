Questions

Frequently asked

Is this actually free?
Yes! Your first compliance document is free, no credit card required. You can run the risk check, see your applicable regulations, answer the questions for one document, and have it emailed to you at zero cost. Subscription plans unlock unlimited documents and automatic update alerts.
How accurate are the generated documents?
3CH0 generates documents by mapping your specific answers against the actual regulatory text, not a fill-in-the-blank template. Documents are built to reflect the regulation clauses that apply to your situation. For complex or high-stakes situations, we always recommend having a legal professional review the output.
I'm based in South Africa. Do I need GDPR?
Possibly. GDPR applies based on where your customers are, not where you're based. If you have even one EU or UK customer, you may have GDPR obligations. Our risk check identifies this automatically and tells you exactly what you need to do about it.
What documents can 3CH0 generate?
Privacy Policy, PAIA Manual (required for SA companies), Data Processing Agreement, Cookie Notice / Consent Banner, DSAR Procedure (GDPR), Business Associate Agreement (HIPAA), Consumer Request Form (CCPA), Breach Notification Policy, and more. The documents shown are tailored to the regulations that apply to your specific business.
What happens when regulations change?
Subscribers receive automatic alerts when regulations that affect their business are updated or enforced. You can regenerate any document with one click to reflect the latest requirements. No need to start from scratch.
Is my business data safe?
Yes. Your answers are used only to generate your document and are not shared with or sold to third parties. Data is processed securely over encrypted connections. We are POPIA-compliant ourselves, which means we practice what we preach.
